Live Zoom Meeting Formation

What is your Live Zoom Formation service and how does it work?

What are the benefits of using the Live Zoom Formation service?

The key benefits include: 1) Complete privacy - all information stays on your device, 2) Enhanced security - no document sharing required, 3) Transparent payments - you pay all government fees directly, 4) Expert real-time guidance throughout the process, 5) Documentation verification - we only charge after confirming your Articles of Organization, and 6) 100% transparency - you see every step of the process as it happens.

How long does a Live Formation session take?

A typical Live Formation session takes approximately 60-90 minutes. During this time, we'll complete all necessary paperwork and submit your formation documents to the appropriate state agencies. The exact duration may vary depending on the complexity of your business structure and the specific state requirements.

What do I need to prepare before a Live Formation session?

Before your session, you should: 1) Have your business name options ready, 2) Decide on your business structure (LLC or C-Corp), 3) Know your preferred state of formation, 4) Have payment methods ready for state filing fees (credit card or ACH), 5) Ensure you have a stable internet connection, and 6) Set aside uninterrupted time for the session. We'll send you a detailed preparation checklist after you schedule your appointment.

Yes, our Live Formation service is available for all 50 US states. Our formation experts are familiar with the specific requirements and processes for each state, ensuring a smooth formation experience regardless of where you choose to register your business.

What happens if we can't complete everything in one session?

If we're unable to complete all formation steps in a single session, we'll schedule a follow-up session at no additional cost. We're committed to ensuring your business formation is completed correctly and thoroughly, even if it requires additional time.

Business Formation

What is the best state to form an LLC in the USA?

Delaware, Wyoming, and Nevada are often considered the best states for LLC formation due to their business-friendly laws, tax benefits, and privacy protections. However, the ideal state depends on your specific business needs, location, and goals.

How long does it take to form an LLC with Infinity Filer?

The LLC formation process typically takes 7-10 business days with Infinity Filer, depending on the state's processing times. We offer expedited services that can reduce this timeframe to 2-3 business days in most states. The actual Live Formation session takes approximately 60-90 minutes, during which we'll complete all the necessary paperwork and submissions.

What's included in your LLC formation package?

Our comprehensive LLC formation package includes: Articles of Organization filing, Operating Agreement, EIN/Tax ID application, Registered Agent service for the first year, compliance calendar, and unlimited customer support. When combined with our Live Formation service, you'll also receive personalized 1:1 guidance throughout the entire process with enhanced privacy and security.

What is the difference between an LLC and a C-Corporation?

An LLC offers pass-through taxation and simpler compliance requirements, while a C-Corporation is a separate tax entity with more complex requirements but better suited for raising capital and going public. C-Corps also offer more robust liability protection and can have unlimited shareholders. During our Live Formation session, we can explain the differences in detail and help you choose the right structure for your business.
